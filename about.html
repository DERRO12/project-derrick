About BBC SACCO

Our Vision

To be the leading member-focused financial cooperative that empowers individuals and businesses to achieve financial independence and prosperity.

Our Mission

To provide innovative financial solutions through savings mobilization and credit facilities, fostering a culture of financial discipline and mutual support among our members.

Our History

BBC SACCO was established in 2010 by a group of dedicated professionals who recognized the need for a member-owned financial institution in our community. Starting with just 50 members, we have grown to serve over 5,000 members with assets exceeding UGX 10 billion.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We conduct our business with the highest ethical standards and transparency.

Member Focus

Our members are at the center of everything we do.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all our services and operations.

Innovation

We continuously improve our services to meet evolving member needs.
