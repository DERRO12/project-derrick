Membership Information

Member Benefits

  • Access to low-interest loans
  • Competitive savings interest rates
  • Annual dividends on shares
  • Voting rights in SACCO decisions
  • Financial education and training
  • Insurance coverage benefits

Membership Requirements

  • Must be 18 years or older
  • Complete membership application form
  • Provide copies of identification documents
  • Purchase at least 5 shares (UGX 10,000 each)
  • Pay membership fee of UGX 20,000
  • Maintain a minimum savings balance

How to Join

  1. Visit any of our branches
  2. Complete the membership application form
  3. Submit required documents
  4. Pay membership fee and purchase shares
  5. Attend new member orientation
  6. Start enjoying member benefits
